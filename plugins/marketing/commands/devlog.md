# Devlog Creator

Create a development log post for sharing progress.

## Why Devlog?

- Builds audience before launch
- Forces useful reflection
- Creates marketing assets over time
- Attracts collaborators and opportunities

## Output Format

```markdown
# Devlog: [Title] - [Date/Number]

## Quick Update (for social media)

```
[Hook: What's interesting this week?]

[Visual: GIF/screenshot description]

[1-2 sentences of context]

[Optional question for engagement]

#gamedev #indiedev #[genre]
```

## Detailed Devlog (for blog/newsletter)

### What I Worked On
[Summary of progress this period - 2-3 paragraphs]

### The Interesting Part
[One challenge, decision, or learning worth discussing in detail]

**The problem:**
[What you faced]

**What I tried:**
[Your approach]

**What I learned:**
[The insight]

### Visual Progress
[Description of screenshots/GIFs to include]

- [Visual 1]: [What it shows]
- [Visual 2]: [What it shows]

### What's Next
[Preview of upcoming work - builds anticipation]

### Numbers (optional)
| Metric | This Period | Total |
|--------|-------------|-------|
| [Metric] | [Value] | [Value] |

---

*[Call to action: Wishlist, follow, join Discord, etc.]*
```

## Content Categories

Mix these to keep content fresh:

**Progress:**
- Before/after comparisons
- New features in action
- Milestone celebrations

**Learning:**
- How you solved a problem
- Design decisions and tradeoffs
- Tools and techniques

**Human:**
- Why you're making this
- Struggles and setbacks
- Genuine reactions to milestones

## Platform Guidance

| Platform | Format | Frequency |
|----------|--------|-----------|
| Twitter/X | Short + GIF | 2-3x/week |
| Reddit | Detailed + discussion | Weekly |
| YouTube | Video walkthrough | Bi-weekly |
| Newsletter | Digest format | Weekly/bi-weekly |

## Don't Share

- Every tiny bug fix
- Negativity about others
- Promises about dates you're not certain of
- Technical details without visual hook
- Complaints without lessons

Generate based on user's recent work.
