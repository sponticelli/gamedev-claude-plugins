# Influencer Brief Generator

Create a comprehensive influencer outreach and collaboration brief for game marketing.

## Context Gathering

Before creating the influencer brief, understand the goals:

### Define Campaign Goals
- What's the primary objective? (awareness, wishlists, sales)
- What's the timeline?
- What's the budget range?
- What metrics matter?

### Understand the Game
- What genre/type of game?
- What's the unique selling point?
- What's the target audience?
- What platforms is it on?

### Check Resources
- What keys/codes are available?
- What assets can be shared?
- What embargo rules exist?
- Who manages relationships?

### Identify Constraints
- What can influencers NOT do?
- What messaging must be included?
- What platforms are priority?
- What regions are targeted?

Use this context to create an appropriate brief.

```markdown
## Outreach Templates

### Initial Contact (Cold)
```
Subject: [Game Name] - Partnership Opportunity

Hi [Name],

[Personal note about their content]

I'm reaching out about [Game Name], a [brief pitch]. Given your
content about [relevant topic], I think it could be a great fit
for your audience.

We're looking for partners for our [launch/campaign] and would
love to discuss working together.

What we can offer:
- [Key benefit 1]
- [Key benefit 2]
- [Key benefit 3]

Would you be interested in learning more?

Best,
[Your name]
[Contact info]
```

### Key Request Response
```
Hi [Name],

Thanks for your interest in [Game Name]!

Here's your key: [KEY]

A few notes:
- [Embargo info if applicable]
- [Any content guidelines]
- [Press kit link]

We'd love to see your content when it's live! Please share
with us at [email/social].

Thanks,
[Your name]
```

### Paid Partnership Proposal
```
Subject: [Game Name] - Sponsored Content Opportunity

Hi [Name],

We'd love to propose a sponsored content partnership for
[Game Name]:

Proposed deliverables:
- [Deliverable 1]
- [Deliverable 2]

Proposed compensation: [Amount/range]

Additional benefits:
- [Benefit 1]
- [Benefit 2]

Interested in discussing further?

Best,
[Your name]
