# One-Page Game Design Pitch

Condense a game concept into a single page that communicates the vision clearly.

## Output Format

```markdown
# [GAME TITLE]
**Genre:** [Genre/Subgenre] | **Platform:** [Target Platforms] | **Players:** [1P/Coop/Multiplayer]

---

## THE HOOK
[2-3 sentences that make someone want to know more. What's unique? What's the fantasy?]

---

## CORE GAMEPLAY
**The Loop:** [One sentence describing the core loop]

**Key Mechanics:**
• [Mechanic 1]: [What player does and why it's fun]
• [Mechanic 2]: [What player does and why it's fun]
• [Mechanic 3]: [What player does and why it's fun]

---

## THE FEEL
**This game is:** [3-5 adjectives that describe the experience]

**Players should feel:** [The core emotional experience]

**A moment that defines the game:** [One specific moment that captures the essence]

---

## SETTING & THEME
[2-3 sentences on the world, visual style, and tone]

---

## REFERENCE GAMES
| Reference | What We Take | Our Twist |
|-----------|-------------|-----------|
| [Game 1] | [Element] | [How we make it ours] |
| [Game 2] | [Element] | [How we make it ours] |

---

## TARGET AUDIENCE
**Primary:** [Who will love this most?]
**Secondary:** [Who else will play?]

**Why they'll care:** [What need does this satisfy for them?]

---

## SCOPE INDICATOR
**Estimated Content:** [Rough scope]
**Team Size:** [Suggested team]
**Complexity:** [Low/Medium/High]
```

## Guidelines

- Every word must earn its place
- Be specific, not generic
- The hook is the most important part
- If you can't say it simply, the concept isn't clear
- Reference games show awareness, not copying

Generate immediately from user's concept.
