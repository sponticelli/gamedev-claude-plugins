# Audio Brief Generator

Create a rapid audio direction brief from a game concept.

## Output Format

```markdown
# Audio Brief: [Game Title]

## Sonic Identity in One Sentence
[The entire audio approach in one line]

## Audio Keywords
[5 adjectives that describe how the game should sound]

## Sound Design Direction
### Player Sounds
- **Feel:** [Descriptors]
- **Example Sounds:** [Key player sounds]

### World Sounds
- **Feel:** [Descriptors]
- **Ambient Character:** [Description]

### UI Sounds
- **Feel:** [Descriptors]
- **Feedback Style:** [Description]

## Music Direction
### Genre/Style
[Musical approach]

### Instrumentation
[What instruments/synths]

### Emotional Range
| Moment | Musical Feel |
|--------|-------------|
| [Moment 1] | [Feeling] |
| [Moment 2] | [Feeling] |

## Reference Audio
| Reference | What to Take |
|-----------|-------------|
| [Game/Film] | [Specific quality] |
| [Game/Film] | [Specific quality] |

## What We're NOT
[Audio approaches to avoid]

## Technical Considerations
- **Platforms:** [Target platforms]
- **Adaptive Needs:** [Music system complexity]
- **VO Requirements:** [Voice over needs]

## First Audio Test
[What single sound would prove this direction works]
```

Generate based on user's game concept.
