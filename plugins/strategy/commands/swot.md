## SWOT Guidelines

**Strengths:** What you do well internally
**Weaknesses:** What you lack internally
**Opportunities:** External factors you can capitalize on
**Threats:** External factors that could hurt you

## Tips for Better Analysis

- Be specific, not generic
- Focus on what's most impactful
- Distinguish internal from external
- Consider different timeframes
- Validate assumptions with data

Apply to user's context.
