## Pitch Tips

- **Lead with the hook** - Capture attention immediately
- **Show, don't tell** - Include visuals and demos if possible
- **Know your numbers** - Be prepared for financial questions
- **Anticipate objections** - Address risks proactively
- **End with clear ask** - Be specific about what you need
